Our Story

Founded in 2017 by a group of passionate scuba divers, OceanDive began as a small dive shop with a big dream: to share the wonder and beauty of the underwater world with everyone.

What started as weekend diving trips for friends has grown into a comprehensive diving education and adventure company, serving thousands of divers from around Switzerland. Our commitment to safety, environmental conservation, and exceptional customer service has made us a trusted name in the diving community.

Today we continue to operate with the same passion and dedication that inspired our founding, always putting our divers' safety and satisfaction first.

Our Core Values

Safety Excellence

We maintain the highest safety standards with regular equipment maintenance, comprehensive training, and experienced dive masters on every trip.

Ocean Conservation

We're dedicated to protecting marine ecosystems through sustainable diving practices and supporting local conservation efforts.

Continuous Learning

Our instructors stay current with the latest diving techniques and safety protocols to provide the best education possible.

Community Focus

We believe diving is better when shared, fostering a welcoming community where divers of all levels can learn and grow together.
